Actions

Fold
Throw your hand away. You lose what you've already bet — but nothing more.
Check
Pass the action. Only if nobody has bet this round.
Call
Match the current bet to stay in the hand.
Raise
Increase the bet. Must be at least the previous raise size.
All-in
Shove every chip you have. Others can call, fold, or raise (if they have more).

Position badges

D
Dealer button — rotates left each hand. In heads-up, acts first preflop.
SB
Small blind — forced half-bet, posted left of the dealer.
BB
Big blind — forced full bet, posted left of SB. Acts last preflop, first after.

Hand rankings

  1. Royal Flush 10-J-Q-K-A, same suit
  2. Straight Flush 5 in a row, same suit
  3. Four of a Kind
  4. Full House three + a pair
  5. Flush 5 same suit
  6. Straight 5 in a row
  7. Three of a Kind
  8. Two Pair
  9. One Pair
  10. High Card

How a hand works

  1. Blinds — SB and BB are posted automatically.
  2. Hole cards — 2 cards dealt to each player (yours show face-up).
  3. Preflop — bet, call, raise, or fold.
  4. Flop — 3 community cards. Another betting round.
  5. Turn — 4th card. Bet again.
  6. River — 5th card. Final bet.
  7. Showdown — best 5-card hand from your 2 + the 5 board cards wins the pot.

Tips

  • Click an empty seat to join the next hand.
  • The brass ring shows whose turn it is.
  • Out of chips? Hit Re-buy for a fresh stack.
  • Want to start over? Reset wipes the whole table.
